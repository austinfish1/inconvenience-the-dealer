Inconvenience The Dealer

The game where you can finally get back at Vegas


Rules

The aim of this game is to guess the value of the top card on the top of the deck. You have 2 attempts to guess the value of the card. One player starts as the dealer, and the next player guesses the top card.

If the player is incorrect, they get to guess again, but first they are told if their original guess was higher or lower than the card. If the player is incorrect on their second guess, they receive points equal to the difference between the value of the card and their second guess. The card is then layed on the playing field and the game moves to the next player.

However, if the player guesses the card correctly on their first try, the dealer receives 4 points and the game moves to the next player. If they are correct on their second try, the dealer receives 2 points.

The dealer cannot pass the deck to the next player until 3 people in a row guess incorrectly. The player with the lowest points at the end of the game is the winner. Oh yeah, and Aces are low.

First enter the number of people playing (that green button below ya doofus) and then click on the deck of cards below to draw a card
